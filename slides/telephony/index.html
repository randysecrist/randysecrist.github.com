Outbound Funnel


What it is?

Currently Operational team collecting data weekly and uploading into InContact. Since they are uploading manually Telephony has no way to validate those records. Since there is no validation is happening that could cause a grief with customer cause they might have done something to their application. Also it engages the operator for no reason. But automating this and validating pre call check in could reduce the load on the Operators also reduces small irritation on the borrower.


What kind of data?

Activity data where the borrower application status could be missing info, prefund and so on.
